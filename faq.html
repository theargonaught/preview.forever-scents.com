Shipping & Orders
Soy wax has a lower melting point than paraffin, so candles shipped in hot summer weather may soften slightly during transit. They will re-harden once cooled and this doesn't affect burn quality. We recommend ordering early in the week so packages aren't sitting in a hot truck over the weekend.
Please allow 5–7 business days from the time your order is placed. All candles and melts are handmade in small batches, so we want to make sure yours is fresh and perfect before it ships.

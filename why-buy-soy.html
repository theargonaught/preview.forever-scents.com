Why Buy a Soy Candle?

Not all candles are created equal. Here's why soy wax makes a real difference for your home, your health, and the planet.

🌱

100% Natural & Renewable

Soy wax is made from soybeans grown right here in the USA. It's a completely renewable resource — unlike paraffin, which is a petroleum byproduct.

🏭

Supports American Farmers

Every soy candle you buy puts money back into American agriculture. Soybeans are one of the biggest crops in the Midwest — we're proud to use them.

🌍

Naturally Biodegradable

Soy wax is biodegradable and water soluble, making cleanup easy and keeping your environmental footprint small.

🫁

Non-Toxic & Clean Burning

Paraffin releases toxic benzene and toluene when burned. Soy wax is non-toxic and produces 90% less soot — a big deal if you have kids, pets, or allergies.

⏱️

Burns Up to 50% Longer

Soy burns at a cooler temperature, which means a slower, more even burn. Our 9 oz candles burn for 40–50 hours. You get more value for your money.

🌸

Wonderful Scent Throw

Soy wax holds fragrance beautifully and releases it evenly throughout the burn — no overpowering burst at the start and nothing at the end.

Why soy wax?

🌱

Natural & Clean

Made from soybeans — a renewable resource. Non-toxic, biodegradable, and water soluble.

⏱️

Burns Longer

Burns up to 50% slower than paraffin. Our 9 oz candles burn 40–50 hours.

💨

90% Less Soot

Dramatically cleaner burn means no black soot on your walls, jars, or in your air.

🇺🇸

Supports Farmers

Soy wax is grown in the USA. Every candle supports American soybean farmers.

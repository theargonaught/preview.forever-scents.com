Market Details
🗓️

Every Saturday

We're at the Ottawa Farmer's Market every Saturday morning through the end of August. Rain or shine!

⏰

Morning Hours

Doors open early — come by in the morning for the best selection. We often sell out of popular bread flavors by midday.

🕯️

What We Bring

Handmade soy candles, wax melts in seasonal & year-round scents, and nine varieties of fresh baked sweet breads.

💬

Custom Orders Welcome

Want a specific scent or bread variety? Contact us ahead of time and we'll have it ready for you at the market.
